Welcome

Content of this Website

This website is based on IMDb, the purpose is to give the opportunity to anyone to add content to a movie description.

To access to the entire site you need to create an account. Once the account created, it's possible to look every movie page ont the website, to post a comment, to rate the movie or add content to the page. It's also posssible to add a movie to the database, there are two options, the first one is to fill a form with the information about the movie, the second one is to add the movie from the IMDb database. You just have to go to the "POST A MOVIE", write the title of the movie, to click on search then click on save to add the movie. Then you can go on the tab "MOVIES" the movie added, and click on this one to have and entire page with the information about the movie. You can like or rate the movie on the right part of this page. On the tab "PROFILE", you can modify some information about you, you can also see the movies that you rated or liked. A section named "PLACES", allows you to add places that you have seen or that you want to see. You can also display a map with markers on those places.

Main functionalities: A user can post a movie either with the form or with IMDb. Can like and rate a movie, he can also add content to the movie page, a simple information or an entire section. On the profile page the user can modify some informations about himself. He can also create a list of places that he wants to see or he has already seen, and can display it on a Google Map. It's also possible to visualise the profile of the others users.

On the movie page, a background poster and a basic poster are displayed, There are some important informationabout the movie. On the right part, there is the rating section, the general rate is display and the user can rate the movie. He can also like the movie. There is also the trailer or a video related to the movie. Below there is the comment section, each comment has a title, and the name of the writer and the date are displayed. The writer can remove the comment at any time. Below there is the form to post a new comment and at the bottom another form can be used to add content to movie page.

I used many APIs, geolocation from Google Maps, OMDb and TMDb in order to get data about movies
